Welcome to Cheap Prime Fares!

We value our customers and believe that great experiences are worth sharing. That’s why we’ve created our Refer and Earn program, giving you the chance to earn rewards for spreading the word about us. Here’s how it works:

**1.** How It Works
• Refer a Friend: Invite your friends, family, or colleagues to book their flights through Cheap Prime Fares. Simply share your unique referral link or code with them.
• They Book, You Earn: When your referral completes a booking using your link or code, you’ll earn rewards! It’s that simple.

**2.** Rewards
• Earn Rewards: For every successful referral, you’ll receive [Specify Reward - e.g., a discount on your next booking, travel credits, or cash rewards]. The more you refer, the more you earn!
• Track Your Earnings: Keep track of your referrals and rewards through your account dashboard. You’ll see how many people have used your referral link and the rewards you’ve accumulated.

**3.** Getting Started
• Sign Up: If you’re not already a member, sign up for our loyalty program to get started. Once you’re a member, you’ll receive your unique referral link or code.
• Share Your Link: Use your referral link or code to invite others to experience the great deals and service at Cheap Prime Fares. Share it via email, social media, or directly with your network.

**4.** Terms and Conditions
• Eligibility: The Refer and Earn program is open to all registered members of Cheap Prime Fares. Referrals must be new customers who have not previously booked with us.
• Reward Activation: Rewards are issued once your referral completes a booking and the transaction is confirmed. Rewards may be subject to certain conditions or restrictions, which will be detailed in your account dashboard.
• Program Changes: Cheap Prime Fares reserves the right to modify or terminate the Refer and Earn program at any time. Any changes will be communicated to participants through their account dashboard or email.

**5.** FAQs
• How do I find my referral link? You can find your unique referral link or code in your account dashboard under the "Refer and Earn" section.
• How can I track my referrals? Log in to your account and visit the "Refer and Earn" section to see the status of your referrals and the rewards you’ve earned.

**6.** Contact Us
• Need Assistance? If you have any questions about the Refer and Earn program or need support, please contact our customer service team. We’re here to help!
