Auli - The Skiing Destination of India


Auli can be found in the Chamoli district in Uttarakhand's northern region. Located in the beautiful state of Uttarakhand, this mountainous region is a part of the Garhwal range. Skiing in Auli is world-famous, and the town itself is a popular tourist destination in Uttarakhand. This beautiful spot is set on a ridge 2800 metres above sea level. Auli famous for its steep oak-lined hills and lush green forests. Some people believe that the great Hindu saint Guru Adi Shankaracharya came to this holy location. Visitors may take in breathtaking vistas of the Nanda Devi, Kamat and Mana Parvat mountain ranges that have boosted Auli tourism.

Best Time to Visit Auli


Auli destination is accessible throughout the year. Most visitors come between November and February when snow skiing is at its peak and the Auli weather is cold. Climate-wise, auli best time to visit is between March and June. The ideal Auli temperature for hanging out with loved ones on a trip and taking in the sights. 


How to Reach the Places to Visit in Auli


Jolly Grant Airport, located 279 kilometres from Auli, is the closest airport and may be reached by cab. Rishikesh Railway Station is the closest train station to Auli. 


Auli has excellent road connections to all major cities in Uttarakhand. From Haridwar/Rishikesh, you may take a state-run bus to Joshimath, where you can catch a cab or ride the ropeway to Auli. You can also opt for a cab to explore the tourist places near Auli Uttarakhand.


What are the things to see and do in Auli? 


The government has created the Auli Artificial Lake specifically to supply artificial snow to the new ski slopes in the months with poor precipitation. Other than that, you can embark on a trek to Gorson Bugyal. You can also spend time exploring nearby tourist spots such as Nandaprayag, Mana Village, Joshimath, Badrinath and more. Hence, there is a lot to see and things to do in Auli.


Places to Visit in Auli


Gorson Bugyal

This Auli tourist place, perched at a height of 2,500-3,500 metres, is a popular trekking destination and a must-see if you wish to see local Garhwali life up close. In the summer, this Auli trek is famous for its verdant meadows.


Auli Artificial Lake

When natural snowfall is scarce, the government constructed this lake to generate artificial snow for the ski resorts. The snow cannons along the ski run get their water supply from the lake.


Narsimha Temple

According to legend, Adi Guru Shankaracharya was the one who established this temple for Lord Narsimha, which is now an important centre of worship for Hindus.


Restaurants in Auli


Al-Fresco Cliff Top Club

The restaurant, Al Fresco Cliff Top Club, is known for its delectable and auli famous food. With delicious cuisine and awe-inspiring views of the surrounding snow-capped mountains, you may have a wonderful evening.


Devi Darshan Lodge

Auli's Devi Darshan Lodge is a great option for lodging. Guests may dine in the hotel's restaurant, which serves gourmet fare that appeals to a wide variety of palates. 


Auli-D Food Plaza

If you're craving some delicious Indian, continental, or Chinese food, head on over to Auli-D Food Plaza. The decor is elegant and reminiscent of a banquet hall.


Adventures in Auli


Ropeway Ride

The ropeway ride is the most exhilarating activity in Auli. After Gulmarg, this is Asia's second-longest and tallest cable car trip.


Camping 

Auli camping is perfect for large groups and should be enjoyed throughout the summer when snow-covered meadows are transformed into lush greenlands.


Skiing

When the slopes of Auli gets covered under a thick blanket of snow, thats when you can have a wonderful time enjoying skiing in Auli with your loved ones.


Places to Stay in Auli


Himalayan Eco Lodge

In the Himalayan Eco Lodge Auli, you may enjoy the stunning panorama of the Himalayas without sacrificing any of the comforts of home. 


Casa Himalaya

This is a luxury ski resort with spacious, private cottages and sweeping vistas of Nanda Devi and the surrounding Himalayas from every room.


Nandan Kanan Hotel

Guests at Nandan Kanan Hotel Auli will be treated to breathtaking vistas of snow-capped mountains, expansive orchards and the tranquil waters of Auli Lake, located just a short drive away. 





Food to Try in Places to Visit in Auli


Some restaurants here offer dishes inspired by the Garhwali culture. Garhwali cuisine is based on a variety of lentils and rice dishes. Kachmauli, singhori, and a plethora of other dishes are among the many delicious specialities of the region. Tea, coffee, and instant noodles can be found at little cafÃ©s along the hill stations streets.


Things to Buy in the Places to Visit in Auli 


Auli Uttarakhand is dotted with several big and small shops where you can shop for everyday needs as well as warm clothes.


Joshimath Market

If you're looking for a great place to do shopping while in Auli, your best bet is Joshimath Market. Warm clothing, woollen hats, handicrafts, souvenirs and shawls can be purchased in Joshimath, which is located about 13 kilometres from Auli India.


JD Jeans Collection Joshimath

If you are looking for high-quality jeans, then you can head to this small shop in Joshimath and find a pair of jeans of your size.


Shubham Gift Center

If you would like to take a souvenir back home, then there can be no better option than this gift shop, offering toys, showpieces and religious idols.
